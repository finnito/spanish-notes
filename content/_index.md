## Genders

### Masculine Nouns

Masculine nouns typically end in

- _o_,
- an accented vowel,
- _ma_,
- a consonant (other than d, z),
- e

## Present Tense

|                     | AR   | ER   | IR   |
|---------------------|------|------|------|
| Yo                  | o    | o    | o    |
| El/Ella             | a    | e    | e    |
| Tu/Usted            | as   | es   | es   |
| Nosotros            | amos | emos | imos |
| Vosotros            | áis  | éis  | ís   |
| Ellos/Ellas/Ustedes | an   | en   | en   |
